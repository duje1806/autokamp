About Us / Since 2001

Your quiet corner under the canopy of century-old olive trees.

Auto-camp "Pod maslinom" is located 11 km west of the most famous city on the Adriatic coast, Dubrovnik, in the beautiful Mediterranean village of Orašac.

Location & Capacity

Feel the Mediterranean in every step.

It occupies about 8000 m2 of area, i.e., 50 camping spots under olive trees, hence the name of the camp. A path through the pine forest leads you in 3 minutes to 2 pebble beaches and a boat dock.

In the village, there are also two mini-markets, a post office, a clinic, bocce courts, a school with football and basketball courts, and a botanical garden. The camp was thoroughly renovated in 2001.
